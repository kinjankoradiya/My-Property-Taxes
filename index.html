About us

Why My Property Taxes?

We’re the leading experts in property tax tracking solutions and have been since 2003. Our flagship software solution was designed to identify delinquent taxes and liens. But our large, investor clients also needed solutions to keep better tabs on their assets. And, since every state and county has different property tax laws and processes for redemption it can be very confusing. That’s why we created MyPropertyTaxes.

When we discovered that more than 30% of delinquent property taxes were because of bank/lender negligence we were shocked! The cost of fees and penalties assessed? A staggering $1.39 Billion (with a B)!

Clearly things are slipping through the cracks…even in the most rigid and secure environments. With that, and we set out to come alongside your systems to provide a simple and affordable way to monitor your assets.
